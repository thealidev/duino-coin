What is Duino-Coin?

Duino was founded in 2019 and is a for-fun, fully original crypto coin project developed by a team of young developers that focuses on energy efficient mining. It's mostly, but not only, dedicated to people who are just starting out in the crypto world as it doesn't require any expensive equipment.

Kolka system

To make mining with underpowered devices possible Duino uses the so-called Kolka system that consists of a few stages that test the device performance, assign a difficulty tier and distribute the rewards according to these variables.
Kolka is explained in more detail in the Whitepaper.

Multi-chain

Duino-Coin is a centralized coin with it's own chain. It's possible however to wrap (convert) DUCOs to wDUCO, bscDUCO, celoDUCO or maticDUCO to store them in a decentralized form on another coins' chain. More info about wrapping can be found in the getting started guides.
